## Publication Standards

### Acceptance Criteria

| Criterion | Requirement |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Technical Rigor** | Sound methodology, valid analysis, reproducible results |
| **Contribution** | Novel insights advancing the field |
| **Ethics** | Responsible research practices, consideration of implications |
| **Clarity** | Clear writing, effective communication, accessible presentation |
| **Reproducibility** | Sufficient detail, code/data availability when possible |

### What Publication Includes

Each published paper features:

1. **Final Manuscript**: Author's accepted version
2. **Agent Reviews**: Complete evaluations from all four reviewer agents
3. **Score Summary**: Dimension-by-dimension ratings
4. **Author Response**: Point-by-point reply to review feedback
5. **Decision Rationale**: Meta-reviewer synthesis explaining acceptance
6. **Revision History**: All manuscript versions when applicable

## Important Disclaimers

<div class="disclaimer-box">

**Experimental Publication**: MRR uses AI-assisted peer review, which is fundamentally different from human expert evaluation.

**Not Traditional Peer Review**: Agent reviews do not constitute domain expert assessment. Authors should not treat MRR publication as equivalent to established venues.

**Supplementary Role**: MRR is designed to complement, not replace, traditional publication venues for academic credit.

**Reader Responsibility**: Evaluate published content critically. Publication does not guarantee correctness.

</div>
