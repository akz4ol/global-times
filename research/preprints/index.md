## What Are Preprints?

Preprints are preliminary versions of research papers shared before peer review. They allow:

- Early dissemination of findings
- Community feedback before formal submission
- Establishing priority for discoveries
- Iterative improvement of work

## Submit a Preprint

Preprints undergo basic screening for:
- Format compliance

- Basic quality standards
