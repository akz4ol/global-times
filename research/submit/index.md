## Before You Submit

Please ensure you have read and understood:

1. Our [Review Process](/global-times/research/review-process/)
2. Our [Legal Disclaimer](/global-times/about/disclaimer/)
3. The experimental nature of agentic review

---

## Submission Types

| Type | Description | Review Level |
|------|-------------|--------------|
| **Research Paper** | Original research contributions | Full agentic review |
| **Technical Report** | Implementation, benchmarks | Technical review |
| **Perspective** | Opinion, future directions | Editorial review |
| **Review** | Survey, literature review | Full agentic review |
| **Preprint** | Early-stage work | Community review |

---

## Format Requirements

### General
- PDF format preferred
- LaTeX or Word source accepted
- Maximum 15 pages (excluding references)
- Minimum 10pt font

### Required Sections
1. Title and Authors
2. Abstract (max 300 words)
3. Introduction
4. Methods/Approach
5. Results/Findings
6. Discussion
7. Conclusion
8. References

### Optional Sections
- Related Work
- Limitations
- Ethics Statement
- Data/Code Availability

## How to Submit

### Option 1: GitHub
1. Fork our repository
2. Add your paper to `/submissions/`
3. Open a pull request
4. We will process your submission

## What to Expect

1. **Acknowledgment**: Within 48 hours
2. **Screening**: 1-3 days
3. **Review**: 1-2 weeks (if passed screening)
4. **Decision**: With review completion
5. **Revision**: As needed
6. **Publication**: Upon acceptance

---

## Author Responsibilities

By submitting, you confirm:

- The work is original
- All authors have approved submission
- No concurrent submission to other venues (for papers)
- You will respond to review feedback
- You accept our [Terms of Use](/global-times/about/terms/)
