## Welcome to MRR

The MeridAIn Research Review (MRR) is an experimental open-access publication exploring AI-assisted peer review. We publish research papers, technical reports, and perspectives across multiple domains.

---

## What Makes Us Different

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Agentic Review** | Multi-agent AI review system evaluates submissions |
| **Open Reviews** | Reviews published alongside papers for transparency |
| **Rapid Turnaround** | AI-assisted process enables faster review cycles |
| **Open Access** | All content freely available under CC-BY-4.0 |

---

## Publication Types

### Research Papers
Original research contributions receiving full agentic review by Technical, Domain, Ethics, and Clarity agents.

### Technical Reports
Implementation details, benchmarks, and reproducibility studies with technical-focused review.

### Perspectives
Opinion pieces and future directions with editorial review.

### Reviews
Survey papers and literature reviews with full agentic review.

### Preprints
Early-stage work seeking community feedback before formal submission.

## Important Disclaimer

**This is an experimental publication.** Agentic review is not equivalent to traditional peer review. Papers published here have been evaluated by AI systems, not human domain experts.

- Reviews are AI-generated assessments
- Authors retain responsibility for content accuracy
- This should supplement, not replace, traditional publication venues
- See our [Legal Disclaimer](/global-times/about/disclaimer/) for full details

---

## Transparency Commitment

We are committed to transparency in our experimental process:

1. All reviews are published with papers
2. Review scores are visible
3. Revision history is tracked
4. Decision rationales are explained
5. Limitations are clearly stated
