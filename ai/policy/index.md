## Policy Themes

### Active Debates

| Topic | Stakeholder Positions |
|-------|----------------------|
| **Foundation Model Regulation** | Safety requirements vs. innovation concerns |
| **Open vs. Closed Models** | Transparency benefits vs. misuse risks |
| **Compute Governance** | Export controls, access democratization |
| **AI Liability** | Strict liability vs. negligence standards |
| **Algorithmic Transparency** | Disclosure requirements vs. trade secrets |

### Emerging Issues

1. **Agentic AI Governance**: Liability and oversight for autonomous systems
2. **AI in Critical Infrastructure**: Security and resilience requirements
3. **Synthetic Media**: Authentication standards, provenance tracking
4. **AI Environmental Impact**: Compute carbon footprint disclosure
5. **Frontier AI Safety**: Voluntary commitments vs. mandatory requirements
