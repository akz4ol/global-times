## Coverage Approach

Our research coverage emphasizes:

1. **Significance Assessment**: Focus on work advancing the field rather than incremental improvements
2. **Reproducibility**: Prioritize papers with available code and detailed methodology
3. **Context**: Position new work within the broader research landscape
4. **Accessibility**: Translate technical contributions for broader audience comprehension
5. **Attribution**: Always link to original papers and author pages
