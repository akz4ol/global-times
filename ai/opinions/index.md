## Editorial Standards

### Our Analytical Approach

| Principle | Implementation |
|-----------|----------------|
| **Evidence-Based** | Claims grounded in research, data, and documented developments |
| **Epistemic Humility** | Explicit acknowledgment of uncertainty and limitations |
| **Multiple Perspectives** | Representation of diverse, well-reasoned viewpoints |
| **Constructive Criticism** | Critique that advances understanding rather than scores points |
| **Independence** | Analysis not influenced by commercial or political interests |

### What We Avoid

- **Hype Amplification**: Uncritical repetition of marketing claims
- **Doom Narratives**: Speculation presented as certainty
- **False Balance**: Treating well-supported and fringe positions equivalently
- **Credential Worship**: Evaluating arguments on merit, not author prestige
- **Recency Bias**: Context from history and precedent

## Reader Guidance

### Interpreting Our Analysis

All analysis and opinion content reflects the views of its authors, which may include AI-assisted synthesis. Readers should:

1. **Consider the Evidence**: Evaluate the supporting arguments and sources
2. **Seek Alternatives**: Read contrary perspectives from other sources
3. **Check Credentials**: Understand author expertise and potential biases
4. **Note Uncertainty**: Pay attention to hedging and confidence levels
5. **Update Beliefs**: Incorporate new information proportionally

### Engaging Critically

We encourage readers to:
- Challenge arguments they find unconvincing
- Identify unstated assumptions
- Consider unaddressed perspectives
- Distinguish prediction from recommendation
- Separate facts from interpretation
