## Competitive Dynamics

### Frontier Model Competition

The AI industry has consolidated around several competing approaches:

| Strategy | Primary Players | Characteristics |
|----------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Closed Frontier** | OpenAI, Anthropic, Google | Proprietary models, API access, enterprise focus |
| **Open Weights** | Meta, Mistral, Alibaba | Released weights, community fine-tuning |
| **Vertical Integration** | Apple, Amazon | Hardware-software optimization, on-device AI |
| **Infrastructure** | NVIDIA, cloud providers | Compute layer, platform services |

### Key Trends

1. **Compute Arms Race**: Unprecedented capital deployment in AI infrastructure
2. **Agentic AI**: Shift from chat interfaces to autonomous task execution
3. **Enterprise Adoption**: Fortune 500 moving from pilots to production
4. **Regulatory Pressure**: Compliance costs rising across jurisdictions
5. **Talent Concentration**: Premium compensation concentrating in frontier labs
